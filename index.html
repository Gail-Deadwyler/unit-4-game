Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal images below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown below and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you when you click on it.

Every new game will change the values of the crystal.
